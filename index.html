Rules

One player controls the White team and the other player controls the Green team. Each team has 5 game tokens: 3 square tokens and 2 round tokens. All 5 tokens can move around the board by traveling on empty spaces (an empty space is one that doesn't have a game token on it). In addition to moving, the square tokens have the power to PUSH one or more tokens one space.

START: Each player positions their tokens on opposite sides of the centre of the board. Most players will put 4 tokens at the center line, but you don't have to do that.

PLAY: Players take turns moving up to 2 tokens into position for an attack, then using one of their square tokens to push one space. You can push tokens to a new space, or you can push one of them off the board to win the game. Move or push forward, backward or sideways (in either direction). NO DIAGONAL MOVES OR PUSHES ARE ALLOWED. A move can travel several spaces. A push is only one space.

WIN: Trap your opponent so you can push one of their tokens off the board at either end. When one token is off the board, the game is over. There are a lot of places at each end of the board where you can push them off.You can also win if your opponent surrenders because he or she is trapped and can't get out.
